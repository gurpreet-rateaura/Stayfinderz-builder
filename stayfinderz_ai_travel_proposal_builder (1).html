Day-Wise Itinerary

DAY 1Arrival in Singapore · Jul 15
Welcome to the Lion City! Arrive at Changi Airport where our representative will greet you. Private coach transfer to your hotel with English-speaking guide. Check-in and spend the evening exploring the vibrant surroundings.
DAY 2Night Safari · Jul 16
An unforgettable evening at Singapore's iconic Night Safari. Enjoy a delicious dinner at The Courtyard Restaurant, followed by a complimentary Animal Show and Tram Ride through the nocturnal wildlife habitats.
DAY 3Experiential Singapore Tour · Jul 17
Full-day guided tour covering Chinatown's temples, Raffles Hotel, Merlion Park, Orchard Road, Esplanade, and more. STB-licensed guide provides rich historical and cultural commentary throughout.
DAY 4Sentosa + Universal Studios · Jul 18
Morning at Sentosa: Madame Tussauds, Cable Car on the Mount Faber Line, Wings of Time (7:40 PM show). Afternoon at Universal Studios Singapore with SGD 20 meal voucher included.
DAY 5Gardens by the Bay & Marina Bay Sands · Jul 19
Visit Marina Bay Sands Skypark Observation Deck for panoramic city views. Then explore the breathtaking Gardens by the Bay — Cloud Forest (Jurassic World Experience) and Flower Dome conservatories.
DAY 6Departure · Jul 20
Leisurely breakfast at hotel. Check-out by standard time. Private coach transfer to Changi Airport with guide. Fond farewell from Singapore — until next time!
